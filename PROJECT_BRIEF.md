# DEFCON 23 - Project Brief

## Overview
Website for technical consulting in systems engineering, compliance architecture, and computational fluid dynamics (CFD) applied to organizational structures.

## Mission
"La carne se pudre. El HUESO permanece. Diseñamos sistemas cuya arquitectura está en la capa de protocolo."

## Target Audience
- CISOs, CTOs, Compliance Officers
- Companies subject to NIS2, DORA, AI Act (EU) + ENS, RGPD (Spain)
- Revenue: €2M-€50M, 10-250 employees

## Services & Pricing
- Initial consultation: €750 (90 min)
- Auditoría de Flujo: €4,500-€12,000
- Hardening: €12,000-€24,000
- CFD Vortex: €8,500+
- Compliance Architecture: €15,000-€35,000

## Core Methodology
Apply Reynolds number (Re) from fluid dynamics to organizations:
- Laminar (Re < 2,300): Ordered, efficient, low friction
- Turbulent (Re > 4,000): Chaotic, high friction, detectable

Our process:
1. Calculate client's Reynolds number
2. Map viscosity (friction points)
3. Apply lamination protocol (7 passes)
4. Harden structure (resistance to audits)
5. Build compliance-native architecture (4 layers)
